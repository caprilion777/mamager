Cookie Policy

Last updated: August 6, 2025

Mamager.ai uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site recognize your browser and store certain information.

How we use cookies

We may use cookies for:

Essential functions – to make our site work properly (e.g., keeping you logged in).

Performance and analytics – to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.

Preferences – to remember your settings and choices.

Mamager.ai does not use cookies to collect sensitive personal information.

Third-party cookies

We may allow trusted service providers (such as analytics tools) to place cookies to help us analyze usage. These providers may also collect information about your browsing activity on other websites.

Your choices

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies through the cookie banner on our site.

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies in their settings. If you block essential cookies, parts of our site may not function properly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
